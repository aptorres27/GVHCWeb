About Golden Valley Health Center

Golden Valley Health Centers [GVHC]

We challenge ourselves everyday to be the premiere healthcare organization in the nation by developing exceptional teams and providing exceptional experiences to every person we serve. We’re a community-based healthcare organization offering medical care, dental care, behavioral health counseling, health education, and more. GVHC is a Joint Commission-Accredited, private, nonprofit Federally Qualified Health Center serving Merced and Stanislaus Counties in California.
